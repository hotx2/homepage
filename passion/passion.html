Historical Chinese Dramas

What is a Historical Chinese Drama?

Historical Chinese dramas are television shows or films that are set in ancient China and often feature historical figures, events, and cultural elements. These dramas typically focus on the lives of emperors, courtiers, warriors, and other historical personalities, often incorporating themes of romance, political intrigue, and moral dilemmas. Some often include elements of fantasy and mythology due to China's rich mythological background.
